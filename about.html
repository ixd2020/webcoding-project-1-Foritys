    • About artist

    His works are often the portrayal of his own unique interests in food, animals and science. His portfolio includes painting, graphic arts, film, sculpture, design and photography, and sometimes collaboration with different artists. He was also interested in writing fiction, poetry, autobiography, essays and criticism. The most common themes for his work includes dreams, subconscious, sexuality, religion, science and his own personal closest relationships. However to the dismay of many who often held his work in high regard, his eccentric and overall public behavior often drew more attention than his artwork towards himself.

    There are currently two major museums that are devoted to showcasing Salvador Dalí's work: the Dalí Theatre-Museum in Figueres, Spain, and the Salvador Dalí Museum in St. Petersburg, Florida.
