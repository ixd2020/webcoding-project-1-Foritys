Artist Description

Salvador Domingo Felipe Jacinto Dalí i Domènech (Or Salvador Deli) was a Spanish surrealist artist renowned for his technical skill, and the striking and bizarre twist and forms of images in his work. From the late 1920s Dalí introduced many interesting and unique images into his work which invite symbolic interpretation.
